Virtual Bazaar aims to build a market system that empower the Consumers to buy from wide variety of Stores which would meet their demands, and also break the monopoly of few Dealers that supply commodities. The Digital Platform will help local Suppliers get their space in Market and also extend their reach to Consumers who didn't notice these Shops. The Platform supports every kind of supplier - whether a food supplier or a mechanic or an electronic dealer or a Cart selling Groceries, everyone have access to Virtual Bazaar, and to buy from anyone and negotiate on what they want is the Freedom the Consumer has.

The Customers will be able to connect with the market digitally and also get wide range of options to buy, thus making trade open access and level in the Market. To provide the service of such a massive scale Market, Machine Learning Model will be applied to regulate the deals between Merchant and the Customers.
Clustering & Association rule learning will be the learning Models to manage the big dataset of Products and Purchase
